## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Evidence-Based Problem Statement
The landing page SHALL feature a dedicated section detailing the "call bell burden," nursing time loss, and the language gap, citing existing literature to establish clinical grounding.

#### Scenario: Problem section visibility
- **WHEN** a visitor scrolls to the "Problem" section
- **THEN** they see descriptions of call bell burden and nursing inefficiencies backed by evidence.

### Requirement: Outcome-Focused Value Proposition
The landing page SHALL clearly display the value proposition: "Nurse arrives prepared. One visit. Any language." as the primary outcome-level solution description.

#### Scenario: Value prop display
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** the outcome-level value proposition is prominently visible in the hero or solution section.

### Requirement: Team Credibility Section
The landing page SHALL include a section with names, photos, and brief bios for the NORA team members to establish founder credibility.

#### Scenario: Team bios accessibility
- **WHEN** a visitor navigates to the "Team" section
- **THEN** they can read the backgrounds of the team behind NORA.

### Requirement: Harvard Innovation Validation
The landing page SHALL prominently display the text: "Australian winners of the Harvard University Health Systems Innovation Lab Global Hackathon" to signal international validation.

#### Scenario: Harvard validation visibility
- **WHEN** a visitor lands on the homepage
- **THEN** the Harvard hackathon win is immediately apparent as a credibility signal.

### Requirement: Pilot Interest Capture
The landing page SHALL provide a contact form or email link specifically for pilot interest, with the label: "Interested in piloting NORA in your facility — get in touch."

#### Scenario: Pilot interest submission
- **WHEN** a visitor fills out and submits the pilot interest form
- **THEN** the system captures their contact information for NORA's follow-up.

### Requirement: "In Development" Framing
The landing page SHALL include "coming soon" or "in development" messaging to set accurate expectations about the product's current state.

#### Scenario: Expectation setting
- **WHEN** browsing the site
- **THEN** the visitor sees clear indicators that NORA is actively being built and not yet publicly deployed.
