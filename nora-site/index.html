The Problem

Healthcare environments face a documented call bell burden, leading to significant nursing time loss and patient safety risks due to the language gap.

Call Bell Burden

High volumes of non-clinical requests disrupt nursing workflows and contribute to burnout.

Nursing Time Loss

Inefficient communication leads to multiple trips for simple tasks, wasting valuable clinical time.

Language Gap

Barriers in communication between patients and providers compromise care quality and safety.

The Outcome

NORA ensures that when a nurse responds, they arrive with exactly what the patient needs. One visit. Any language.
