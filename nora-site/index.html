The Problem

Healthcare environments face a documented "call bell burden," leading to significant nursing time loss and patient safety risks.

🔔

Call Bell Burden

Studies indicate up to 50% of call bells are for non-clinical needs, creating constant cognitive load and "alarm fatigue."

Source: Journal of Clinical Nursing
⏱️

Nursing Time Loss

Nurses lose an average of 2.5 hours per shift due to communication inefficiencies and multiple trips for simple patient requests.

Source: Health Care Management Review
🌐

Language Gap

Patients with limited English proficiency face a 30% higher risk of adverse clinical events due to communication barriers.

Source: Joint Commission Resources

The Solution

A clinical workflow that anticipates patient needs before the first step into the room.
